How You Can Help?

In these troubling times, we are all trying to find ways to help given the humanitarian catastrophe unravelling in Eastern Europe following the Russian Invasion in Ukraine. Considering our background and expertise, we may contribute to alleviate the enormous toll on mental health for both Ukrainian refugees and Ukrainian civilians trapped in the war zone.

The Federation Global Initiative on Psychiatry (FGIP) is an international federation of non-profit organizations promoting ethical mental health around the world. It was founded in 1980 as the International Association on the Political Use of Psychiatry and, over the years, it coordinated the campaigns against the political abuse of psychiatry in the Soviet Union and several other countries. Since 1989, it became involved in developing ethical mental health services in Central and Eastern Europe and the former USSR.
